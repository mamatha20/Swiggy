History of Swiggy

Swiggy Food Delivery Harsha mentioned in an interview that he started discussing opportunities and business ideas with Nandan Reddy and both of them found great potential in online services and commerce industry in general. He gave an example involving Amazon, Flipkart, e-Bay as successful e-commerce platforms. It was clear to both the co-founders that they wanted to do something which was technology-based and something which worked offline as well. Therefore, Swiggy is a perfect blend of both these features.
